Upload Files

With the upcoming deprecation of the Rich Tier on cloud, the file upload functionality intoduced in v95 of PAW is a great addition.

Instructions

Step 1: Navigate to the workbench, right click on the relevant database. Click on File Manager.

Step 2: Click the upload button, this will bring the file upload dialog box up, you can drag and drop your files here.

Step 3: Once you click upload, the file will be transferred to the following location on the TM1 server and be available for use by your Turbo Integrator processes as normal.
